Wisp

A wisp is a small, elusive, and often ethereal creature that is commonly found in folklore and mythology. It is typically depicted as a glowing or shimmering entity, often associated with light or fire. Wisps are often said to be mischievous or playful, leading travelers astray or guiding them to hidden treasures. They are believed to be spirits of the dead or supernatural beings that inhabit forests, swamps, or other natural environments. Wisps are often described as being small and delicate, with a faint glow that can be seen in the darkness. They are often associated with mystery and enchantment, and their presence is said to bring both wonder and danger.
